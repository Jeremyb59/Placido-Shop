Calculation of the withdrawal period:

You have a minimum of 14 days to change your mind about your remote purchase.
The 14-day period is the minimum legal period.
The professional can offer a longer period to his clients.
The right of withdrawal also applies if the product is on sale,
second-hand or destocked.
You can exercise your right of withdrawal from the date of delivery.
