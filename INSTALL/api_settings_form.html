App settings :

* Note: All of the following settings can be modified from your management interface after installation.

Your website :




Your management account :

- You access your management interface by the following address,
ex. : https://my-website.com/ADMIN
You simply add /[your_admin_folder_name] to the end of your website address.
We recommend that you do not keep the "ADMIN" folder name present during installation.





App Mailbox & Public email :

The application needs a mailbox to send automatic emails.
This mailbox will serve as a tool to send emails through the app.
Use the parameters of the SMTP method (outgoing),
they will be provided to you by the hosting service of your mailbox.

 
 
 
 

We advise you to work with several mailboxes,
for your security and for the needs of the application.


App languages :



To translate the public and private interfaces of the application
you must have the [lang].txt files present in the /translate and /[YOUR_ADMIN_FOLDER]/translate folders.
- If you cannot find your language in these folders,
create your own translation files respecting the standard: [2-letter language code].txt e.g.: en.txt, fr.txt, etc ...
See : How to translate Placido-Shop interfaces
See also the Placido-Shop downloads page for download translations.
